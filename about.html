About

Co-Founder @ VBuild

AI-Integrated Product Engineer building products from idea to launch.

The Story

I didn't take the traditional path.

I started building websites without a Computer Science degree. No bootcamp. No formal training. Just curiosity, relentless practice, and a drive to create things that work.

Over time, I served 30+ clients, worked with 7+ international clients across different time zones, and generated $600+ in revenue through projects. Each project taught me something new — not just about code, but about what makes a product actually useful.

I completed a Full Stack Development Internship at Internee.pk, worked through freelance roles at BrightCraft Digital Ltd, NovaLynx Tech Solutions Inc, and SkyNest Innovations Pty Ltd — and eventually co-founded VBuild, a product-focused software agency.

Today, as Co-Founder of VBuild, I build SaaS products, work with AI-assisted workflows, and help founders turn ideas into launch-ready products. I think in systems, not just features.
